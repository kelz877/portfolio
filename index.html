About

Let me introduce myself.

I'm a Full Stack Software Developer currently living in Houston, Texas. I grew up overseas in England and Egypt and moved to California to attend Santa Clara University. I graduated with degrees in Political Science and Psychology and headed to Denver, Colorado. I lived in Denver and worked as a School Counselor for several years earning a Masters of Arts in Counseling Psychology. I have an interest in understanding the issues that young people face and helping them to empower themselves to overcome obstacles and reach their goals.

After many years in education, I decided I was ready for a new challenge. I have always loved technology and science, so web development felt like a perfect fit for my transition. I finished the 2019 school year and enrolled in DigitalCrafts' four month, full-time immersive software development bootcamp in June.
